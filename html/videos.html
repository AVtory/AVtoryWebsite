Adaptive Sports & Recreation Association 2015 from ra creations studio on Vimeo.

Camera-- Canon EOS 5D Mark III &Nikon D90 35mm
2015-- Spent the week around San Diego with The Adaptive Sports & Recreation Association’s. Their mission is to improve the quality of life for children and adults with physical disabilities through sports and recreation. ASRA works to coordinate and foster a program of adaptive sports for individuals with a permanent physical disability that prevents them from participating in sports and athletics by providing competitive league sports, recreational team sports, instructional camps, sports clinics, and offers other adaptive sports based on need and interest.
I helped with filming and capturing the camp, using Canon EOS 5D Mark III & Nikon D90 35mm, Laura Marquez also helped in filming, she operated the goPro. Editing was done by ra creations studio .



YWCA 2014 -- Recap footage from YWCA Walk a Mile in Her Shoes // San Diego 2014 on Youtube.

Camera-- Canon EOS 5D Mark III & Nikon D90 35mm
Editing Software-- Adobe Premiere.
2015-- The YWCA of San Diego County empowers women, children and families to break the cycle of domestic violence and homelessness and achieve self-sufficiency. Through Becky’s House®, Passages and the Cortez Hill Family Center, the YWCA provides extensive services to more than 6,000 individuals each year. The YWCA is committed to helping families recover from trauma, improve their economic status, find permanent housing and ultimately transform their lives.
I helped with filming and capturing this walk down in San Diego, Gaslamp. It is always great working with nonprofits to help spread awareness on the issue of domestic violence.
